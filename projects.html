About Me

I am a Computational Biology graduate student from IIIT-Dehli, working on my thesis with Dr.Vinod Scaria from IGIB on Pharmacogenomic analysis of Arab populations. I did my undergrad in engineering with Biotechnology as my major from JIIT.

Research Interests

I am interested in studying the genetic basis of drug response [Pharmacogenomics], how it varies in different populations [Precision Medicine] and among different individuals [Personalized Medicine].I have worked on Next generation sequencing data to analyze mutations and find novel ones. I have also used automated, semi-automated and manual processes to curate databases including: Database for ACMG incidental findings and Database for disease variants in Arab countries.

  • Naiive Bayes Classifier for gene expression data

    Mar’17-Apr'17

    Advisor: Dr. Vibhor Kumar

    A Naive Bayesian classifier was developed which categorized gene-expression dataset into Control (Target value= 1) and Case (Target Value =0) class. The data was normalized to fit the range [0,1] and gaussian distribution was used for calculating the posterior probability. The accuracy of the classifier for the given dataset was 60 - 70% keeping in account that the test and training data wass randomly selected from the corpus. An approximation which did not allow R to approximate very small values to 0 or large values to infinity was chosen which increased the accuracy between 70-80%. Feature selection using PCA was done and the top 10 features were used which showed no significant imporvement in the accuracy as the dataset was very noisy.
