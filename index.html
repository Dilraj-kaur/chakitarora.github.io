I am a research scholar currently doing my Masters in technology from Indraprastha Institute of Informatiion Technology, Delhi. I am currently working on my thesis research on Pharmacogenomic analysis of Arab populations with Dr.Vinod Scaria from IGIB (Institute of Genomics and Integrative Biology).

I have done my B.Tech in Biotechnology from Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Noida, where I gained experience in performing wet-lab experiments and an aptitude for research.

About Me

I graduated in Biotechnology from JIIT in 2015. During the 4 years of study, I worked in the field of microbiology- by studying bioremediation of inorganic pollutants using genetically engineered bacteria, and health science- by studying the role of prebiotics and probiotics in various diseases. I did my dissertation in my final year in the area of microbiology and molecular biology - to study of bacteriocin-like-inhibitory substances on bacterial biofilms.

With the knowledge of biology, I started my master's degree in Computational Biology. With subjects like, data science and genomics, I gained interest in Pharmacogenomics and precision medicine, which are the focus areas of my thesis on- Pharmacogenomic analysis of Arab population.

Research Interests

  • Pharmacogenomics
  • Precision Medicine
  • Genomic/Exomic data interpretation
  • Disease Database curation
