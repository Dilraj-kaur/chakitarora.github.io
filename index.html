About Me

I am a Computational Biology graduate student from IIIT-Dehli, working on my thesis with Dr.Vinod Scaria from IGIB on Pharmacogenomic analysis of Arab populations. I did my undergrad in engineering with Biotechnology as my major from JIIT.

Research Interests

I am interested in studying the genetic basis of drug response [Pharmacogenomics], how it varies in different populations [Precision Medicine] and among different individuals [Personalized Medicine].I have worked on Next generation sequencing data to analyze mutations and find novel ones. I have also used automated, semi-automated and manual processes to curate databases including: Database for ACMG incidental findings and Database for disease variants in Arab countries.
